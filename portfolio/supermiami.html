SuperMiami

OVERVIEW

SuperMiami is a top-down shooter which combines aspects of Hotline Miami (top-down shooter) with Superhot (time within the game only progresses when the player moves).

This project was developed together with the Game Development Society of Imperial College London. SuperMiami was primarily developed in GameMaker by a team of 2 people. I provided sprites for the visuals and programmed the enemy AI as well as various other minor features.
